## Why a V2?

The original Unojoy wheel worked, but it had limitations: no >180 degrees turn, limited buttons, and a clunky pedal connection. V2 is a ground-up redesign fixing all three.

## The Build

The new design uses a more capable potentiometer, a switch joycon for the buttons, and a cleaner wiring harness for the pedal set.

## Skills Demonstrated

Advanced Arduino/C++ programming, analog and digital I/O, PCB layout, and iterative hardware design.
