Today I was formally inducted into the National Junior Honor Society. NJHS isn't just another club to me — it's the part of school that quietly holds the community together, from candy grams to homework help.

I'm excited to help lead homework-help sessions and to pitch two ideas I care about: a FIRST Lego League club and a school e-waste recycling drive.
